Research Interests
======
My research interests lie at the intersection of fairness, evaluation, and alignment in large language and multimodal models. I focus on understanding how existing evaluation paradigms fail to capture socially grounded harms, particularly in settings involving ambiguity, subjective judgment, and real-world deployment across text, vision, and multimodal inputs.
